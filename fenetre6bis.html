Fast Forward
english©

Fast Forward English is a website developped by Matta Karczewski and Samy Salamor for and english project at Télécom Nancy (France). It’s purpose is to show a fun way to learn english using what everyone loves to do, aka, watching interesting videos on the internet.
After extended research, we found that millenials spent the most of their time surfing the internet looking at various content. This website uses the growing habits of our population to best target the learning process.
New content is regularly added, keeping up to date is one of the core issues we tried to address when thinking in ways of learning english.
You will find exercices about most topics on the website but if you don’t find your favorite topic, do not hesitate to contact us !
We’ll make sure to get right on that !
